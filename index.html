About Me

My name is Bo Katsarov. I was born in Bulgaria and moved to the U.S. when I was 15 to go to a tennis academy. I competed in the Junior U.S. Open and later played Division I tennis for UC Berkeley and graduated with a degree in Business Administration. Today, I am a software engineer. Technology was always something I was very passionate about throughout my life. I get up every morning trying to make the most out of my opportunity to learn new technologies and find solutions to problems. I have experience in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, Python, Django, React.js, Redux and jQuery.
